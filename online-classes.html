Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need for online classes?

A yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and a reliable internet connection are all you need. Props like blocks and straps are helpful but not required.

How do I join the live classes?

After booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with a secure link to join the class. Simply click the link a few minutes before the class starts.

Can I access recordings if I miss a live class?

Yes! All live classes are recorded and available for 7 days after the session for registered participants.

Do you offer private sessions?

Yes, private sessions are available for those seeking personalized guidance. These sessions are tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Is there a community aspect to the online classes?

Absolutely! We have a private online community where students can connect, share experiences, and support each other on their yoga journey.
