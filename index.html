Meet Marta Metrass

I'm a heart-driven yoga teacher dedicated to helping you transform your life through the power of emotional yoga. With years of experience and a deep understanding of yogic traditions, I've developed the Yoga of Emotions® method to guide you on a journey of self-discovery and healing.

My mission is to empower you to connect with your inner wisdom and unlock your limitless potential. Through online courses and transformative retreats, I create a nurturing environment where true transformation can occur.

"Love is above all things. When we embrace our spiritual hearts and cultivate love and kindness towards ourselves and others, we change our lives and contribute to changing the world."
