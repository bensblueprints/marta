Frequently Asked Questions

What airport should I fly into?

You should fly into Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) in Denpasar, Bali. Airport transfers to our retreat center in Ubud are included in your package.

Do I need a visa for Bali?

Many countries receive a 30-day visa on arrival in Bali. We recommend checking the current visa requirements for your country before booking your flights.

What is the payment schedule?

A $99 deposit reserves your spot. 50% of the remaining balance is due 90 days before the retreat, and the final payment is due 60 days before the retreat begins.

What if I need to cancel?

Your $99 deposit is fully refundable up to 90 days before the retreat. Cancellations 60-90 days before receive a 50% refund (minus deposit). Cancellations within 60 days are non-refundable, but may be transferred to another person.

Is this retreat suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! The Yoga of Emotions® method is accessible to all levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Modifications will be offered for all practices.
